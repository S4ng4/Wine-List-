The Art of Balance: Pairing by Weight

The fundamental rule for pairing is simple: Match the weight (or Body) of the wine to the weight of your dish. We use the Olive Oil Analogy to help you understand this:

Light Body

Mouth-Feel: Like a delicate, Light Olive Oil. Very refreshing.

Pairing: Seafood with lemon, light soups, salads.

Medium Body

Mouth-Feel: Like a Standard Olive Oil. Versatile and balanced.

Pairing: Tomato-based pasta, grilled chicken, risotto.

Full Body

Mouth-Feel: Like a Robust Extra Virgin Oil. Dense and complex.

Pairing: Steak, lamb, game meats, rich sauces.
